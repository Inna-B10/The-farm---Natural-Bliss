Embrace Nature's Symphony at Natural Bliss Farm!

Welcome to our cozy farm Natural Bliss, where every visit is a Natural Delight!

We take pride in offering a unique experience suitable for all ages, perfect for relaxation and entertainment.

At Natural Bliss, we open our doors to those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life and enjoy nature. You can feed our friendly animals, participate in farm activities, and feel the joy of connecting with the natural world.

For our little visitors, we have special attractions! Children can enjoy horseback riding, explore farm life, and engage in exciting games designed just for them.

And for our guests who appreciate natural products, we have a lovely farm shop where you have the opportunity to purchase a delightful selection of fresh and organic goods that are grown right here at Natural Bliss.

Everyone, from young kids to seniors, get the opportunity to explore the world of farming and enjoy up-close interactions with adorable animals.

Come and meet the cute goats bouncing around, the chickens cackling merrily, and a cat with cuddly kittens.

Guided program

Join us as we have a fantastic time feeding and petting our adorable sheep and fluffy rabbits. We'd also be delighted to introduce you to our friendly pigs with piglets, geese, ducks, and other animals. Throughout your visit, we'll happily share fascinating facts and stories about each of our lovable friends. Talk about how we can all work together to take care of our environment and make sure rural life remains interesting and vibrant.

The guest experience a farm in daily operation. You also get the chance to pick eggs, feed animals and enjoy both their company and new and exciting experiences.

Adventures with horses

There will be children's horse-riding and horse-and-carriage rides between 12:00-16:00 (additional fee). With us, well-being and security are the focus, and our steady and calm horses are ready to give your kids experiences of a lifetime!

Additional benefits

  • There is an open cafe with sausage, waffles, soda, juice, coffee, tea and cocoa
  • WC and baby changing rooms are available
  • You'll also find an awesome playground with cool cabins and swings.
  • Nice walking areas in a quiet environment without cars or noise
  • It is also possible to rent boats on the farm (additional fee)
  • A farm shop where you can purchase fresh and organic goods grown or made at Natural Bliss: eggs, cheese, milk, fresh herbs, some vegetables, fruits and jams. As well as items made from natural wool.

On a note

  • Remember to dress according to the weather! Boots/waterproof shoes are always recommended, as we tend to go out on the field which can be both wet and muddy.
  • We place great emphasis on the well-being and safety of all visitors. Let's have a fantastic time together both with us and with each other!
